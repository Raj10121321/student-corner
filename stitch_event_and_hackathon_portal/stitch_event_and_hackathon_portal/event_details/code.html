About the Event

Push the boundaries of what's possible with large language models and generative agents. Neural Frontiers is a 48-hour intensive hackathon designed for senior engineers and researchers looking to bridge the gap between theoretical AI and practical application.

Participants will have access to private compute clusters, cutting-edge APIs from our partners, and mentorship from lead architects at major AI labs. Whether you're building autonomous coding assistants or revolutionizing creative workflows, this is the stage to showcase your vision.
